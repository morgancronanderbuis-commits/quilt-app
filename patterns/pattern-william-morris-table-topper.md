# Pattern: William Morris Table Topper
*Moda Fabrics — inspired by the Victoria and Albert Museum archives*

**Finished Size**: 18" x 36"

---

## Fabric Requirements

| Material | Details |
|---|---|
| William Morris 2017 charm pack | #7300PP |
| Moda Grunge charm pack (background/tan) | #30150-162PP |
| Binding | ¼ yard William Morris 2017 (#7307-24) |

---

## Cutting

- Using a ruler and rotary cutter, cut all **84 charm squares twice diagonally** to make a total of **336 quarter square triangles**.
- Cut **3 – 2½" x width of fabric (WOF)** strips for binding.

---

## Construction

Each hourglass block requires **2 background (tan)** and **2 print quarter square triangles**.

### Step 1 — Make Pairs
Stitch all triangles into pairs using 1 print and 1 background triangle. Always stitch pairs with the **print on the right** and the **background on the left**. Make **168 pairs**. Press seams open.

### Step 2 — Make Hourglass Blocks
Randomly select 2 pairs. Stitch together to make 1 hourglass block. Press seam open. Do **not** stitch matching prints together in the same block. Trim blocks to **3½" x 3½"** with seams. Make **84 blocks**.

### Step 3 — Assemble Table Topper
1. Sort blocks into sets of 4 with 1 matching print in each group of four.
2. Position the four matching print triangles toward the **center of the block** to form a pinwheel. Press seams toward prints. Make **21 pinwheel blocks**. You will use 18 of the 21 blocks.
3. Arrange pinwheel blocks into **3 rows of 6**. Stitch together. Press seams in alternate directions.
4. Join rows to complete table topper. Press. Topper should measure **18½" x 36½"** with seams.

---

## Finishing

1. Using a diagonal seam, join binding strips end to end. Press seams open. Fold prepared strip in half wrong sides together and press to make the binding.
2. Layer, quilt, and bind.
