# Pattern: Charm Monday
*Moda Fabrics*

**Finished Size**: 58" x 83"
**Seam Allowance**: ¼" (included in all instructions)
**WOF**: approximately 42"

> Charm Square Tally: Light **164** · Dark **186**

---

## Fabric Requirements

| Material | Amount |
|---|---|
| Charm packs | 9 packs |
| OR light fat quarters | 14 |
| Dark fat quarters | 16 |
| Binding | ⅝ yard |
| Backing | 5 yards |

*The number of squares and the number of light and dark fabrics in a charm pack varies with each collection. Fabric requirements assume charm square packs have 40 – 5"x5" squares and an equal mix of light and dark fabrics. From each fat quarter cut 3 – 5"x22" strips, then cut 12 – 5"x5" squares.*

---

## Cutting

### Light Charm Squares
- From 70: cut **140 – 2½" x 4½"** rectangles
- From 70: cut **280 – 2½" x 2½"** squares

### Dark Charm Squares
- From 70: cut **140 – 2½" x 4½"** rectangles
- From 116: make **116 "pieced" squares** (see below)

### Binding
- Cut **8 – 2½" x WOF** strips

---

## Making "Pieced" Charms

1. Place two 5"x5" squares right sides together.
2. Stitch a scant ⅛" seam on outside edges.
3. Cut in half.
4. Press open to make **2 "pieced" charms** — 4½" x 5" with seams.

**Make 116 pieced charms.**

---

## Block Construction

### Block A — Make 58
- Combine 2 "pieced" squares.
- Press in one direction.
- **Finished size: 5" x 8½" with seams**

### Block B — Make 35
1. Sew 1 – 2½" x 2½" **light** square on the diagonal to 1 – 2½" x 4½" **dark** rectangle. Press to the corner to make a triangle.
2. Sew another 2½" x 2½" **light** square on the diagonal to the **same** rectangle. Press to corner — this makes one flying geese unit. **Make 140.**
3. Add 1 – 2½" x 4½" **light** rectangle to each flying geese unit. **Make 140.**
4. Combine 4 of these units to make **35 of Block B**.
- **Finished size: 8½" x 8½" with seams**

---

## Assembly

Two rows alternate to make the quilt top:

**Row One**: Alternately sew together 4 Block As and 5 Block Bs. Press away from Block Bs. Make **7 of Row One**.

**Row Two**: Alternately sew 5 Block As and 4 – 5"x5" light charm squares together. Press away from the 5" squares. Make **6 of Row Two**.

Join the rows. Layer, quilt as desired, and bind.

**ENJOY!**
