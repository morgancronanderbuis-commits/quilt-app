# Pattern: One Charm Pack Plus...
*Moda Fabrics — Prairie Bailey collection*

**Finished Size**: 36" x 36"
**Seam Allowance**: ¼" (included in all instructions)
**WOF**: approximately 42"

---

## Fabric Requirements

| Material | Amount |
|---|---|
| One Charm Pack | 40 – 5"x5" squares |
| Inner border | ⅓ yard |
| Final border & binding | 1 yard |
| Backing | 1¼ yards |

---

## Cutting

### Inner Border
- Cut **4 – 2" x 27½"** strips

### Final Border & Binding
- Cut **4 – 3¾" x 27½"** strips
- Cut **4 – 2½" x WOF** strips for binding

---

## Construction

### Step 1 — Quilt Center
Sew together six rows of 5"x5" squares using the 40 squares from the charm pack. Press the rows in opposite directions. Sew the rows together.

### Step 2 — Borders
To make the borders: sew one 2" x 27½" inner border strip to one 3¾" x 27½" final border strip. Make 4 borders.

### Step 3 — Assemble
Assemble the quilt top: use the four 5"x5" squares (corner blocks), the four borders, and the quilt center to assemble the quilt.
