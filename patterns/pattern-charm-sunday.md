# Pattern: Charm Sunday
*Moda Fabrics*

**Finished Size**: 64" x 87½"
**Seam Allowance**: ¼" (included in all instructions)
**WOF**: approximately 42"

> Charm Square Tally: Light **197** · Dark **249**

---

## Fabric Requirements

| Material | Amount |
|---|---|
| Charm packs | 12 packs |
| OR light fat quarters | 24 |
| Dark fat quarters | 21 |
| Binding | ⅝ yard |
| Backing | 6 yards |

*Note: If fat quarters are used instead of charms, cut an additional 35 – 5"x5" lights and 15 darks.*

*From each fat quarter cut 3 – 5"x22" strips, then cut 12 – 5"x5" squares.*

---

## Cutting

### Light Charm Squares
- From 4: cut **4 – 4½" x 4½"** (#1 squares)
- From 18: cut **18 – 4½" x 5"** (#2 rectangles)
- From 60: cut **120 – 2½" x 4½"** rectangles
- From 74: cut **296 – 2½" x 2½"** squares

### Dark Charm Squares
- From 130: cut **260 – 2½" x 5"** rectangles
- From 44: cut **88 – 2½" x 4½"** rectangles
- From 60: cut **240 – 2½" x 2½"** squares

### Binding
- Cut **8 – 2½" x WOF** strips

> **Important — size placement:**
> - #1 = 4½" x 4½"
> - #2 = 4½" x 5"
> - #3 = 5" x 5"

---

## Block Construction

### Step 1 — Half Square Triangle Blocks — Make 30
1. Draw a diagonal line on the wrong side of 15 – 5"x5" **light** charm squares.
2. Layer each square with the diagonal line and a **dark** 5"x5" square, right sides together.
3. Sew ¼" on each side of the diagonal line.
4. Cut apart on the diagonal line. Press open.
- **Make 30 half square triangle blocks. Finished size: 4½" x 4½" with seams.**

### Step 2 — Block A — Make 65
- Combine 4 – 2½" x 5" **dark** rectangles. Press in one direction.
- **Finished size: 5" x 8½" with seams**

### Step 3 — Block B — Make 22
1. Sew 1 – 2½" x 2½" **light** square on the diagonal to 1 – 2½" x 4½" **dark** rectangle. Press to corner to make a triangle.
2. Sew another 2½" x 2½" **light** square on the diagonal to the same rectangle. Press to corner — one flying geese unit. **Make 88.**
3. Sew together 2 of the flying geese units. **Make 44.**
4. Combine 2 of these units to make **22 of Block B**.
- **Finished size: 4½" x 8½" with seams**

### Step 4 — Block C — Make 30
1. Sew 1 – 2½" x 2½" **dark** square on the diagonal to 1 – 2½" x 4½" **light** rectangle. Press to corner to make a triangle.
2. Sew another 2½" x 2½" **dark** square on the diagonal to the same rectangle. Press to corner — one flying geese unit. **Make 120.**
3. Combine 4 flying geese units with 1 half square triangle block and 4 – 2½" x 2½" **light** squares to make **30 of Block C**.
- **Finished size: 8½" x 8½" with seams**

---

## Assembly

Four different rows make up the quilt top:

| Row | Contents | Qty |
|---|---|---|
| Row One | Sew together 8 Block As | Make 2 |
| Row Two | Alternate 6 – 4½"x4½" light squares (#1) and 5 Block Bs | Make 2 |
| Row Three | Sew together 2 Block Bs, 4 Block As, and 5 Block Cs | Make 6 |
| Row Four | Alternate 6 – 5"x5" light squares (#3) and 5 Block Bs | Make 5 |

Sew together the rows. Layer, quilt as desired, and bind.

**ENJOY!**
